Beef Bolognese

Beef Bolognese Plate

This recipe doesn't take a lot of ingredients (or a lot of money) to make this classic Bolognese recipe! But it does take is, patience with the sauce to achieve the ideal authentic texture. One thing is, you are going to enjoy making this recipe for the one's you love!

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 1 small carrot, peeled, chopped
  • 3 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 lb. ground beef chuck (20% fat), patted dry
  • 3 oz. thinly sliced pancetta, finely chopped
  • kosher salt
  • 3 oz. thinly sliced pancetta, finely chopped
  • 1 cup dry white whine
  • 1/3 cup tomato paste
  • 1 bay leaf
  • pinch of finely grated nutmeg
  • 2 cups (or more) homemade chicken stock or low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 lb. fresh taggliatelle or pappardelle, or dry rigatoni
  • 2 oz. finely grated Parmesan (about 1/2 cup), plus more for serving
